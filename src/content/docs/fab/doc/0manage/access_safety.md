# Access and safety

For safety, we divide different members who own different rights: 
Visitor, Graduate, Supervisor
If you want to do a project, you can apply for a tool box for your project, and we attach the link: [Tool box application](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/16YrZSg6tmvkSPjY2PUaWezGl4p_KxTKG7eFvPwa2ddA/edit?usp=sharing).

For every person in the lab, before you leave, make sure the lab is safe for everyone:

* If your project is not finished and needs to continue after today,
    * You can put your project in your tool box, and return tools to the lab cabinet (remind: not in your tool box);
    * If the project is too big to fit in the tool box, you need to discuss with the supervisor about storage location;
* If you finish your project, clean up your workspace, return the tool box, and update the [Tool box application form](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/16YrZSg6tmvkSPjY2PUaWezGl4p_KxTKG7eFvPwa2ddA/edit?usp=sharing)

<font color=#ff0000 size=14 face="黑体">Remind: don't arbitrarily put your project on your working space, as it will affect lab management. The supervisor will handle them.</font>



## For visitor
1. Definition: The member who is NexPCB's staff but not a graduate from NexMaker academy. Additionally, this includes customers, suppliers, and NexPCB's friends; 
2. Rights: 
    * You can apply to attend NexMaker academy and become a graduate;
    * If you want to visit the lab or work on a project (design, manufacture, test), you need to apply with the supervisor via email (details in "For supervisor" section);
    * Visitors cannot use 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, drilling machines, or grinders.
    * <font color=#ff0000 size=14 face="黑体">If you encounter any potential danger or emergency, you can use first aid kits (we will add soon) and inform the supervisor as soon as possible</font>

3. Duty: Keep safety
4. Open time: Only during NexPCB's working hours, and you need supervision from a supervisor;

## For graduate
1. Definition: The member who is a graduate from NexMaker academy.
2. Rights:
    * You can get access to the door key;
    * You can use all machines without supervision during working hours;
    * You can apply for materials and equipment [(Reference "material")](material.md)
    * <font color=#ff0000 size=14 face="黑体">If you encounter any potential danger or emergency, you can use first aid kits (we will add soon) and inform the supervisor as soon as possible</font>
3. Duties:
    * You need to send an application email to the supervisor and get approval if you want to use the lab after working hours;
    * You need to get the supervisor's permission if you want to use 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, drilling machines, or grinders;
    * Clean your workspace after working, and throw rubbish in the bin;
    * If your project needs to continue, you need to:
        * Put your project in the specified tool box; if the tool box can't hold your project, you can discuss with Bob;
        * Return tools to their original position;
4. Open time: 
    * Normally during NexPCB's working hours;
    * If you want to work outside of working hours, please use email to request supervisor approval;

## For supervisor

1. Definition: The member who is the administrator of the lab, now two members [Bob:bob@nexpcb.com](bob@nexpcb.com) and [Jacen:jacen@nexpcb.com](jacen@nexpcb.com)
2. Rights: 
    *   Supervisor can use any machine in the lab;
    *   Supervisor have right to change the access of member(add or delete)
    *   <font color=#ff0000 size=14 face="黑体">If you encounter any potential danger, use the first aid kit (we will add soon) and inform another supervisor immediately.</font>

3. Duty 
    * Keep the safety of lab;
    * Keep the clean of lab;
    * Confirm the condition of door and windows;
    * <font color=#ff0000 size=14 face="黑体">You need support dangerous condition as soon as possbile;</font>
4. Open time: You can go to lab anytime you need;
